Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Grounded Beef (80/20)
  • Philly Cheese

Steps:

  1. Find a ceramic or metal tray of your choice for the lasagna
  2. Pre heat at 260F then remove and let rest
  3. After 5 minutes add a layer of butter to the entire tray surface
  4. Start placing the pasta all over the tray as the 1st layer
  5. Add a layer of grounded beef
  6. Add a layer of mozarella cheese
  7. Add a layer of philly cheese
  8. Repeat steps 4 through 6
  9. Add a final layer of pasta at the top layer
  10. Place the tray at the pre heated oven and let cook for 105 minutes at 260F
